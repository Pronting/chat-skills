# Case Studies

## Case 1: Stranger opener on a dating app

Context:

- she has 3 photos and a coffee emoji in bio
- no prior messages

Weak move:

- "在吗"

Better move:

- "你简介里只放了一个咖啡，是认真爱喝，还是懒得写字但不想显得太高冷？"

Why:

- specific
- playful
- easy to answer

## Case 2: She sounds tired, not cold

Context:

- she says: "最近有点忙，懒得说话"

Weak move:

- "是不是对我没兴趣"

Better move:

- "收到，你现在更像电量低，不像不想聊。你先忙，等你缓过来我再正常找你。"

Why:

- labels emotion without accusation
- lowers pressure
- protects momentum

## Case 3: Invite after stable rhythm

Context:

- several days of good back-and-forth
- she asks about your neighborhood

Weak move:

- "你什么时候有空出来见我"

Better move:

- "那你都问到这了，改天别只在线上了解了。这周末找家安静点的店坐坐，方便就去。"

Why:

- uses existing thread
- gives a concrete next step
- keeps refusal easy

## Case 4: Repair after an over-push

Context:

- user sent a flirt line
- she replied with a short "哈哈"

Weak move:

- "你是不是害羞了"

Better move:

- "我刚那句有点飘了，先收回来。你今天像是在忙，我不硬聊。"

Why:

- reduces pressure
- shows calibration

## Case 5: Clean exit after clear refusal

Context:

- she says: "我觉得我们不太合适"

Weak move:

- "你再给我一次机会"

Better move:

- "明白，那我就不继续打扰了。谢谢你直接说，祝你之后顺利。"

Why:

- respects boundary
- preserves dignity
