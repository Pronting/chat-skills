# Red Flags

## Hard no-go zones

Do not advise:

- coercion
- emotional blackmail
- jealousy engineering
- humiliation or negging
- lying about identity, money, status, or intent
- persistence after a clear no
- sexual escalation without clear enthusiasm

## Common male failure modes

### Neediness spiral

Signs:

- double or triple texting with no new value
- asking "why are you ignoring me"
- reacting to delayed replies as rejection

Correction:

- reduce frequency
- shorten messages
- return only with a cleaner thread

### Performance mode

Signs:

- trying too hard to sound witty
- stacking lines with no listening
- acting like every turn must be impressive

Correction:

- return to observation
- ask fewer questions
- build from what she actually gave

### Premature escalation

Signs:

- sexual innuendo with low comfort
- invite before stable rhythm
- exclusivity talk after one good interaction

Correction:

- step back one stage
- rebuild ease and reciprocity

### Adversarial mindset

Signs:

- treating every reply as a test
- assuming bad faith
- obsessing over hidden meaning

Correction:

- prefer behavioral evidence
- tolerate ambiguity longer
- keep one simple next move

## Stop conditions

Advise disengagement when:

- she says no clearly
- she repeatedly avoids all effort
- the interaction only continues through pressure
- the user wants tactics that override consent or dignity
