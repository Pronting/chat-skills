# Conversation Operators

Use these as reusable building blocks. Combine them based on stage and reciprocity.

## 1. Opener

Pattern:

- observation + low-pressure question

Example shape:

- "你这个头像不像随便选的，你平时审美挺挑吧？"

Why it works:

- specific
- easy to answer
- not instantly needy

## 2. Thread Expansion

Pattern:

- take one detail she gave + expand sideways

Example shape:

- "你说你最近总加班，我更想知道你是忙到没空吃饭，还是忙到连脾气都变好了。"

Why it works:

- shows listening
- creates texture beyond Q&A

## 3. Emotional Labeling

Pattern:

- reflect her likely state without overclaiming certainty

Example shape:

- "你这句话听着像不是生气，更像有点懒得解释了。"

Why it works:

- makes her feel understood
- invites correction without pressure

## 4. Specific Compliment

Pattern:

- notice a trait, not just appearance

Example shape:

- "你不是那种一直很热闹的人，但聊天挺有自己的节奏。"

Why it works:

- feels less generic
- creates identity-level recognition

## 5. Playful Push-Pull

Pattern:

- small contrast + gentle tease + no insult

Example shape:

- "你看着挺高冷，结果开口还是有点可爱。"

Why it works:

- creates tension without hostility

Avoid:

- attacking appearance
- comparing her to other women
- implying low worth

## 6. Reciprocity Check

Pattern:

- offer a prompt that requires a little investment, then watch whether she takes it

Example shape:

- "行，那你也说一个我这种人最容易踩的坑。"

Why it works:

- tests engagement without begging for attention

## 7. Invite

Pattern:

- concrete activity + concrete time band + easy refusal

Example shape:

- "你这种更适合线下聊，本周末找家安静一点的店喝杯东西，方便就去，不方便下次。"

Why it works:

- direct
- low drama
- preserves face

## 8. Repair

Pattern:

- acknowledge awkwardness + reset tone + shorten

Example shape:

- "刚才那句有点用力过猛，我收一下。你今天先忙，改天我正常跟你聊。"

Why it works:

- restores ease
- shows self-awareness

## 9. Graceful Exit

Pattern:

- respect + closure + no guilt

Example shape:

- "收到，我就不继续打扰了。祝你这阵子顺利。"

Why it works:

- keeps dignity
- avoids spiral behavior

## Operator selection heuristics

- low reciprocity: use opener, thread expansion, graceful exit
- medium reciprocity: use specific compliment, emotional labeling, reciprocity check
- high reciprocity: use playful push-pull, invite, callback
- fragile situation: use repair before escalation
